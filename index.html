S.T.E.M-Learning system

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. These four disciplines are integrated into the STEM education system to provide a comprehensive and interdisciplinary approach to learning and problem-solving.

SCIENCES

Science is the systematic study of the natural world through observation, experimentation, and analysis. It encompasses various fields such as biology, chemistry and physics.

TECHNOLOGIES

Technology refers to the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes. It includes tools, machines, systems, and processes designed to solve problems.

ENGINEERING

Engineering involves the application of scientific and mathematical principles to design, develop, and build structures, machines, systems, and processes.

MATHS

Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, patterns, and relationships. It involves using logic and reasoning to solve problems and understand abstract concepts.
